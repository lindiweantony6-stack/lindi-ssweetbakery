Our Story

From a Home Kitchen to Pretoria's Favourite Bakery

Lindi's Sweet Bakes started in 2020 in a small home kitchen in Arcadia, Pretoria. What began as a passion for baking birthday cakes for family and friends quickly grew into a fully-fledged boutique bakery serving customers across Pretoria and Gauteng.

Our founder, Lindiwe, believed that every person deserved a beautifully handcrafted baked good made with real, quality ingredients — not mass-produced, not frozen, but made fresh with love every single day.

Today, Lindi's Sweet Bakes operates from our Arcadia bakery and the Hatfield Farmers Market every Saturday, serving hundreds of happy customers weekly.

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌿 Local First

We source all our ingredients from trusted local South African suppliers to support our community and ensure freshness.

❤️ Made with Love

Every item is handcrafted in small batches. We never mass-produce — every bake gets our full attention and care.

✨ Quality Always

We use only real butter, free-range eggs, and premium flour. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no compromise.

🤝 Community

We are proud to be part of the Pretoria community. We support local events, markets, and school fundraisers.
