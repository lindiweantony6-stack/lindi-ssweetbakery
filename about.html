Our Story

From a home kitchen to Pretoria's favourite artisan bakery

How It All Began

A Passion Baked into Every Crumb

Lindi's Sweet Bakes was born in a small kitchen in Arcadia, Pretoria, where founder Lindiwe Mthethwa first began baking custom cakes for family and friends. What started as a heartfelt hobby quickly turned into something much bigger, as word spread through the neighbourhood about her exceptional baked goods.

Fuelled by an unwavering love for the craft and a deep commitment to using only the finest locally sourced South African ingredients, Lindiwe turned her passion into a flourishing boutique bakery. Today, Lindi's Sweet Bakes is a beloved fixture in the Pretoria community, known for quality, creativity, and warmth in every product.

From beautifully crafted custom celebration cakes to freshly baked artisan sourdough breads and delicate pastries, every item that leaves our kitchen is made with intention, care, and a whole lot of love.

Behind the Apron

Meet the Baker

👩🏾‍🍳

Lindiwe Mthethwa

Founder & Head Baker

Lindiwe's love affair with baking began in her grandmother's kitchen in Soweto, where she learned that food is the ultimate expression of love. After completing a pastry arts programme in Johannesburg, she returned to Pretoria with a vision: to create an artisan bakery that felt warm, welcoming, and deeply rooted in local community spirit.

With over six years of professional baking experience, Lindiwe specialises in custom celebration cakes and French-inspired pastries, drawing on both traditional South African flavours and modern European techniques to create something truly unique.
